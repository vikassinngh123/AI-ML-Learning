# 🐼 04-Pandas: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

1. 120 Years of Olympic History (EDA)
A comprehensive study of athlete demographics and performance trends from 1896 to 2022.

The "Host Bump" Analysis: It's prove that host countries see a significant spike in medal counts (e.g., China 2008, Australia 2000).

Gender Parity: Visualized the steady exponential growth of female participation in Summer Games.

Physical Profiles: Analyzed the correlation between Height/Weight across different sports, identifying unique "Physical Footprints" for athletes like gymnasts vs. basketball players.

Age Evolution: Tracked the average age of medalists over 120 years, filtering for Summer Games to remove data "sawtooth" noise.

2. Chips Experimental Practice
Focused on business-style data manipulation using a retail dataset.

Techniques: Applied rolling windows, sales rankings, and data grouping to understand consumer behavior and product performance.
